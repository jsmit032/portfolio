About Me

I Am dedicated

September 2014 I put my life on hold to take a 12-week immersive computer programming bootcamp at General Assembly. Five days a week from 9am - 5:30pm I spent in the classroom learning javascript, ruby on rails, and node. More often than not I would not leave GA until 9:00pm, latest 10:30pm (when GA closes). We were alotted four 3-week time spans to build 4 projects, our first assignment being a rock, paper, scissors built using vanilla javascript. Currenly I'm working on a node back-end to develop a really interactive portoflio, until I release my final product product please hover over my image below for my social media links.
